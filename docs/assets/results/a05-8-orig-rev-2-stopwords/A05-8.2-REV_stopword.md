## A05-8.2-REV 新增停用詞

| 停用詞類型 | 原因 | 停用詞 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 口語與泛用敘事殘留 | A06-8.1 仍出現 `right`、`going`、`want`、`make`、`years`、`important` 等詞，會讓大型 topic 變成逐字稿互動或泛敘事，而不是 EV 子題。 | right, going, want, make, years, important, comes, day, question, watch, videos, photos, hi, stage |
| 駕駛者與一般駕乘敘事 | `driver`、`drivers`、`drive` 相關 topic 中有大量一般試駕敘事；保留 EV drivetrain 相關詞，但補掉泛用駕駛者與非技術駕乘詞。 | driver, drivers, ride, roads, take, no |
| 內裝空間、舒適與便利配備 | A06-8.1 仍分出 comfort、cargo、passenger、legroom、heated、moonroof、trim 等內裝與便利配備 topic，非 EV 技術核心。 | comfort, comfortable, cargo, passenger, legroom, little, modern, heated, moonroof, trim, convenience, outlet, cord, cable, qi, keyless, fob |
| 車機、導航、影音與介面 | `auto`、`radio`、`infotainment`、`maps`、`tablet`、`jbl`、`bose` 等仍形成車機/影音 topic。 | auto, radio, connectivity, infotainment, access, remotely, user, tablet, maps, jbl, bose, watts, wi, fi, menu, camera, destination, directions, sensors, head |
| ADAS、停車與安全細項 | A05-8.1 已移除 broad ADAS 詞，但 A06-8.1 仍保留 `spot`、`park`、`monitor`、`cross`、`cruise`、`collision`、`airbags` 等細項。 | spot, park, monitor, cross, distance, cruise, collision, airbags, brake, oncoming, avoid, safest |
| 外觀、套件與展示語境 | 仍有外觀細節、套件、年份、上市展示與非 EV 產品敘事詞。 | 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021, 2025, 328, 580, 918, a4, i5, i7, x6, byd, sentra, kicks, avalon, niro, sportage, optima, sportback, v8, turbocharged, minivans, hatchback, segment, mode, modes, grade, premium, flagship, le |
| 設計流程、品牌文化與市場展示 | `prototype/prototypes`、`student/culture`、`program/proposals`、`heritage` 等讓 topic 偏向品牌敘事或設計流程。 | prototype, prototypes, culture, student, doubt, trust, program, proposals, proportions, heritage, art, fiction, vr, studio, started, deliver, promotional, leader, inventory, manufacturing, market |
| 其他非 EV 車評與展示殘留 | 車評細節或場景詞仍反覆出現，但與 EV 技術、能源、充電、電池、電驅關係弱。 | top, soft, pins, underneath, wheelbase, problems, pressure, launch, launched, powerful, hassle, shanghai, thomas, tom, italy, prix, turin, group, munich, factory, europe, japanese, scandinavian, lincoln, andy |
