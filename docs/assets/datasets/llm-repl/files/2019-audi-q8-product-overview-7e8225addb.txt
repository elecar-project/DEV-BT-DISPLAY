To be a brand's flagship, there must be something unique, something special, that makes it so.
With the emotional styling of a coupe and the versatility of a sport utility vehicle,
the innovative Brand Model is the flagship SUV.
The flat roofline, wide body, and frameless side windows add to the personality of the Brand Model
and contribute to its powerful stature, all while providing generous headroom.
The octagon grille with contoured air inlets and available HD matrix design LED headlights
add an aggressive look to the Model. The black underlay in the taillight bar pays homage to
the Urquattro, connecting past innovations with present. The 3.0-liter V6 engine in the Model boasts
an impressive 335 horsepower, 369 pound-feet of torque, and can go from 0 to 60 in 5.6 seconds.
Adaptive, damper-controlled suspension is standard, and the available air suspension makes the
Brand Model ready for any road.
The available all-wheel steering system helps connect the driver to the road.
Rear wheel steering can turn the rear wheels up to five degrees, decreasing turn radius,
easing parking, and increasing agility at higher speeds.
Inside the Model, the cockpit reveals the care and attention designers and engineers have
taken to tailor every detail around the driver's needs and comfort. Seamlessly
integrated into the gloss black panel stretching across the dashboard, the MMI
Touch Response System's two screens angle toward the driver and use a
smartphone-like interface. Available exterior cameras and a host of sensors
combine their information and can project a real-time 3D model of the Model
in its environment on the infotainment screen, providing views never before
possible. Brand 's available driver assistance systems continue to grow with
the available adaptive cruise assist, intersection assist, and pre-sense 360.
The rare combination of versatility, strength, and innovative elegance. The
Brand Model.
