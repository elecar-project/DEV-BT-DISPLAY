Thank you, Mr. Van Duyven, Mayor Robison and Mr. Kawase, and good morning, everyone. I'm honored to be here on this special day.
Ladies and gentlemen, these are challenging times, but they are also exciting times. Today I'm going to speak briefly about Brand Motors. Then I will talk about the zero-emission IEM electric vehicle, a car of future that is here today.
Brand Motors Sales of Canada is a young company here in Canada, only 6.5 years old. However, our products are well accepted by Canadian customers and we are growing rapidly. We are very proud of what we accomplished in last year, 2008, with new dealerships opening and the Brand Motor brand gaining recognition.
We ended 2008 with 11.2% sales increase, despite the fact that the overall market was down by 1.1%. And we are keeping that momentum going this year. As of the end of March, we are up by 5.5% on a year-to-date basis, while total industry demand was down by 21.8%. Our March 2009 sales volume of 1,983 units was our best month ever, a new record and 8.9% up over March 2008.
So we are very optimistic in this challenging environment. It is now time to look ahead with courage and conviction. We recognize the need to invest in a bright future and the need to continue bringing right-for-the-times products to the Canadian market. Products like zero-emission electric vehicles you see here.
The IEM is not a concept car. It is a practical, reaching battery-powered electric vehicle that will go on sale this summer in Japan. Brand Motors directed the IEM because of consumer interest, not because of government mandates. The IEM seats four adults with surprising interior room and cargo space.
Other features include excellent acceleration and low center of gravity, which contributes to superior handling and stability. In terms of range, the IMIB is capable of traveling up to 120 kilometers on a single charge, and it is very quiet inside. The charge time is 14 hours using 110 volts or 7 hours using 220 volts. And with an appropriate quick charging station, the I.V. can pick up 80% charging in as little as 30 minutes.
At Brand Motors, we are committed to green technologies and to leading the way in electric car development. The IEM represents the Pinacro Brand Motor's green technologies. We believe in this car and its potential and in its future.
To finish, I would like to again thank you, Mrs. Van Bruven, Mayor Robertson, Mr. Kawase, and I'd like to thank you, Minister Dijkstra, for his involvement in this initiative. We are honored that you have accepted our pollution-free vehicles for evaluation and testing. I'd also like to thank you, City of Vancouver, BC Hydro and the Province of British Columbia and Brand Canada, for your forward thinking, students, and demonstrated commitment to small, efficient vehicles. You are ready to become a leader in consumer acceptance of electric vehicle technology. Thank you very much.
