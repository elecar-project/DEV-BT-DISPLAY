The Model Gran Turismo features an eye-catching retractable active rear spoiler, the first ever for a Brand. This advanced feature gives a sporty appearance and reduces lift on the rear axle by more than 35%. It automatically extends at roughly 65 miles per hour and will retract if the speed drops below approximately 40 miles per hour. The active rear spoiler can be manually overridden by the driver. The controls are located here on the driver side door panel. To extend the active rear spoiler, simply press this button once and the spoiler will fully extend. However, to retract the active rear spoiler, you must press this button and continue holding it until the light goes out, signaling that the spoiler has fully retracted. If you manually extend the rear spoiler, please take care to return it to the down position before entering a car wash to avoid possible damage.
The Model Gran Turismo's cargo cover can be conveniently stored in the floor compartment of the trunk thanks to its expanded cargo area. To do this, first you must remove the cover behind the rear seats. Place your hands in the recessed grips on the bottom of the cover and push upwards until the cover comes free. Next, open the floor compartment by pulling upward on the handle. The cargo cover can then be placed inside like this. Next, remove the cover attached to the tailgate. Place your hands around the lower edge of the cover and pull to unclip. Then push it upwards to disengage and place the cover under the floor compartment. As a last step, simply close the compartment. To put the cargo cover back in place, simply slide the edge of the interior cover over these two retaining pins and then fold it downwards until it engages. Then slide the edge of the tailgate cover into position and pull it downwards to engage. Finally push the cover upwards until it is clipped in position.
The rear seat backrests of the Model Gran Turismo can be adjusted forward to boost cargo capacity or backwards to increase passenger comfort. All told, they offer 15 preset adjustment positions and can recline up to a 19 degree angle. To move the rear backrests, pull up on this handle and then adjust the backrest to the desired angle. When satisfied, release the handle and make sure the backrest locks in place.
The rear seats also offer a cargo function to boost convenience even more. Before using this feature, first make sure the rear seats are free of obstructions and passengers. Next, pull this handle and the backrest will move into its most vertical position. With this loading position, trunk space is maximized while still allowing for rear seat passengers.
The Model Gran Turismo's rear seats can also be folded flat, either separately or together, thanks to their 40-20-40 design. This can be accomplished either using the adjustment handles on the rear seats or via these handles in the cargo compartment. Simply pull up on the lever and then allow the backrests to fold completely forward. When returning the rear seats to their initial position, be careful that the closing path is clear to avoid pinching and then manually raise the backrests into position, making sure they lock in place.
