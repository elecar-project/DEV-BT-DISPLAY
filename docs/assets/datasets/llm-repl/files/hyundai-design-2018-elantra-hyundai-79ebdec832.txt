Introducing the 2018 Brand Model. Designed to give you what you really want in a compact: more. The Model 's exterior displays an evolution of the design language Brand has become famous for, called fluidic sculpture, to create a new, more refined expression called dynamic precision, which aims to blend aspects of dynamism with a sense of balance. The thoughtfulness we've given to the outside of the Model continues inside, where you'll find contemporary and intuitive interior design along with a total interior volume of 110 cubic feet. Forward-thinking design is also on display in the Model 's available HID headlights with dynamic bending light to help you see around corners. Available door handle approach lights help make entry at night easier, and the Model 's available LED daytime running lights and taillights help increase visibility to other drivers.
