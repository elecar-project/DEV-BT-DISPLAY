The new Brand Model is a combination of the brand's innovative luxury and safety, wrapped in a bold design and geared by ultra-efficient new powertrains. A new genre urban crossover with a compact design, bold and stylish. The Model has a unique Brand identity, deeply rooted in the YET philosophy. YET is about combining seemingly opposite concepts into innovative solutions.
For Model, it means a design providing the sense of security of a crossover, yet the agile handling of a hatchback. Model is stylish yet functional, with its wheel arches, rear combination lights, and wheels all having an aerodynamic function. Dynamic yet practical.
Thanks to its low centre of gravity, Model provides excellent driving performance, and this is all made possible thanks to the new TAC platform implemented by Brand for the first time with Model, giving Model fantastic handling agility, ride comfort, and a fun driving personality. Model is also equipped with brand new powertrains, smooth and responsive yet very fuel efficient.
Model comes with second-generation suite of advanced safety features, Brand Safety System Plus, and is already open for the first orders. First delivery is expected early next year. Very exciting!
