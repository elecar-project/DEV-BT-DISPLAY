From the exterior, the new Model models are immediately recognizable as Brand vehicles, and therefore as sports cars.
Efficiency, that is high power coupled with low fuel consumption, is also typically Brand. How does this work? With Brand Intelligent Performance. The Model and the Model 4 are equipped with a newly developed 3.6 liter naturally aspirated V6 engine. It delivers 220 kilowatts, 300 horsepower at 6200 RPM, and maximum torque is 400 Nm at 4250 rpm.
With four individual seats and an elevated center console, the interior of the Model models allows you to travel in sporty comfort. Extensive opportunities for personalization such as two-tone equipment or other combinations of colors and materials add an individual touch. The standard CDR31 audio system with 10 speakers and a total power of 100 watts is quick and easy to control via the 7-inch color touchscreen. Available as an option is the Bose surround sound system with 14 speakers and total power of 585 watts. Or the Burmester high-end surround sound system featuring over 1000 watts of power and 16 speakers for excellent sound quality.
Brand Communication Management PCM, including navigation module, is available as an option. The rear seats are split folding, giving luggage space of up to 1,263 litres.
