The Brand Model is one of the newest members of the Brand family. Only about 10 feet long, this two-door micro subcompact is incredibly efficient and innovative in its design. Notice how the A-pillars are pushed far forward and the side windows are large to help create an airy passenger cabin. And there are lots of cool details, like halogen headlights with unique projector low beams.
The Model 's alloy finish side mirror housings are equipped with turn indicators and include a convenient folding feature that's perfect for tight spots. Check out the three-tier taillight design, a red lens at the top and two clear lenses below with an amber bulb in the center. The Model offers two different wheel cover options for the standard 16-inch steel wheels at no extra charge. Alloy wheels are optional.
Under the hood, there's a 1.3 liter engine with 94 horsepower and 89 foot pounds of torque. It features dual variable valve timing with intelligence, dual VVT-I, that constantly adjusts the intake and exhaust cams throughout the rev range for best performance. The Model 's lightweight CVT automatic transmission not only saves weight, it also enhances fuel efficiency by providing a gear ratio that's ideal for the driving conditions from moment to moment.
The Model has EPA estimated fuel economy of 36 mpg in the city and 37 mpg on the highway, and it does it using regular gas.
Plus, the Model meets the ultra-low emissions vehicle standards set by the California Model Resources Board, which are even stricter than the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency federal standards. To complement its sporting side and its ultra-efficient packaging, the Model rides on McPherson strut front suspension and compact lightweight torsion beam rear suspension. Like all Scions, the Model uses electric power steering, or EPS.
It gives the steering a precise feel and helps save fuel. The turning radius is just 12.9 feet, so the Model is just the ticket for crowded urban driving. With its hatchback design, the Model also offers impressive utility in a micro-sized package. Plus, the Model 's rear seats can split 50-50 or fold flat to create enough space for several pieces of soft luggage. So, let's slide into the Model 's driver's seat.
