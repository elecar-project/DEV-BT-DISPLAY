Brand Model Prime is the ultimate expression of plug-in hybrid electric vehicle capability. Energetic performance comes paired with distinct eye-catching looks and technology designed to keep occupants connected and safe on the road.
For 2024, Model Prime returns with plenty of athletic flair and all the substance that made Brand an electrification pioneer. Offered in three sport-oriented grades consisting of SE, XSE, and XSE Premium, this iconic plug-in hybrid EV is all about fun and turning heads while retaining the outstanding efficiency associated with the Model name. Let's take a deep dive and discover what makes Model Prime such a standout.
Model Prime is all about performance, and that starts with the fifth-generation Brand Hybrid system. This plug-in hybrid EV powertrain couples a two-liter, four-cylinder engine with two electric motors and a high-capacity lithium-ion battery. As a result, Model Prime has 220 net combined horsepower, enabling it to go from zero to 60 miles per hour in as low as 6.6 seconds.
That's not all. Model Prime's fully independent suspension, rigid body, and low center of gravity make it more agile than ever, raising its fun-to-drive factor to new heights. Best of all, efficiency remains impressive. Thanks to the high-capacity battery, Model Prime has an EPA-estimated all-electric driving range of up to 44 miles.
And when operating as a hybrid, Model Prime's EPA-estimated rating of up to 52 miles per gallon combined ensures that the fuel stops are few, allowing the adventures to go on even longer.
Boasting a flowing silhouette and a striking front fascia accentuated by standard LED exterior lighting, Model Prime makes a bold statement. Additionally, standard 17-inch alloy wheels and available 19-inch wheels enhance Model Prime's sporty appearance while hinting at its athletic performance.
Once inside, Model Prime's well-appointed cabin takes comfort and convenience to a new level. This starts with a standard heated steering wheel on all grades. On the other hand, XSE brings SofTex-trimmed heated front seats with 8-way power adjustment for the driver, while XSE Premium adds ventilated front seats, two-position memory for the driver, and available heated outboard rear seats. Furthermore, Model Prime has 20.3 cubic feet of cargo volume, which can be expanded via the standard 60-40 split fold-down rear seats, making it as practical as it is stylish.
Model Prime's clean interior seamlessly integrates the latest multimedia and convenience features. This starts with a top-mount 7-inch multi-information display that keeps crucial information in the driver's sightlines. That's complemented by the latest Brand Audio Multimedia interface, which has a standard 8-inch touchscreen or an available 12.3-inch touchscreen. In either case, the latest system includes standard wireless compatibility for Apple CarPlay, which connects drivers to the apps on their iPhone, including maps, messages, podcasts, and music subscriptions.
In addition, wireless Android Auto compatibility makes it easy to stay connected to Android devices and access various apps and services from Google, including YouTube Music and Google Maps. Plus, there's a standard three-month SiriusXM PlatinumPlan trial subscription that includes over 165 channels available in-cabin, and even more on the SXM mobile app, making it easier than ever to enjoy not just ad-free music, but also sports, comedy, and more. Model Prime also includes trials to Brand 's line of connected services, including Safety Connect, Service Connect, Remote Connect, Wi-Fi Connect, and Drive Connect, which includes cloud navigation, destination assist, and intelligent assistant.
On the other hand, an available 8-speaker JBL Premium audio system plays tunes with vivid Model, while six USB-C ports and available slotted Qi-compatible wireless charging keep mobile devices charged on long road trips.
Model Prime brings peace of mind thanks to Brand Safety Sense 3.0, a standard safety suite that includes Pre-Collision System with Pedestrian Detection, Lane Departure Alert with Steering Assist, Automatic High Beams, Full Speed Range Dynamic Radar Cruise Control, Lane Tracing Assist, Road Sign Assist, and Proactive Driving Assist. The standard Blind Spot Monitor with Rear Cross-Traffic Alert and available Front and Rear Parking Assist with Automatic Braking add an extra layer of convenience on the road, while the available Panoramic View Monitor helps the driver gain a 360-degree perspective around the vehicle. Finally, with the Drive Connect Subscription, Traffic Jam Assist takes convenience to another level. By controlling the vehicle's steering, braking, and acceleration, hands-free operation is possible with driver supervision under 25 mph on controlled-access highways.
