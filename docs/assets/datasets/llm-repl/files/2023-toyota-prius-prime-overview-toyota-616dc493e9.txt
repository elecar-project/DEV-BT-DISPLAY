Brand 's electrification leadership has made it a pioneer, and this expertise is in full display in the all-new 2023 Model Prime. With a totally updated design, exciting performance, outstanding efficiency, and enhanced technology.
Model Prime redefines what a plug-in hybrid can offer. With a sport-oriented lineup consisting of SE, XSE and XSE Premium, drivers can easily pick the Model Prime that fits the needs of their lifestyle. Here's a closer look at what sets this segment standout apart.
Model Prime is purpose-built to perform, thanks to its Plug-in Hybrid System. Powered by the fifth-generation Brand Hybrid System, this Plug-in Hybrid powertrain produces 220 net combined horsepower with a two-liter engine, two electric motors, and a newly developed lithium-ion battery with greater capacity than prior generations. And because it's a Prime model, this Plug-in Hybrid can move, too, with a zero-to-sixty time of 6.6 seconds.
Built on the second-generation TNGAC platform, Model Prime has a lower center of gravity with reduced weight and increased rigidity versus the previous generation. As a result, Model Prime is more agile than ever, making it more exciting from behind the wheel.
In terms of efficiency, the updated battery increases Model Prime's all-electric driving range to a manufacturer-estimated rating of up to 44 miles. If it isn't charged, Model Prime simply operates as an efficient hybrid with a manufacturer-estimated rating of up to 52 miles per gallon combined.
With one look at its new, flowing silhouette, it's clear Model Prime is here to make a bold statement with its latest generation. This starts with a striking front-end design accentuated by standard LED lighting across the lineup. The sportier theme continues with a reconfigured roof peak that's been moved further back, plus an overall vehicle design that's roughly 2 inches lower, 1 inch wider, and 1 inch longer than the previous generation. To bring some extra style to this enhanced stance, SE comes standard with 17-inch alloy wheels, while XSE and XSE Premium step this up to standard 19-inch wheels. For added form and function up top, a fixed glass roof is optional on XSE and standard on XSE Premium.
And just in case of a rainy day, standard rain-sensing wipers can help drivers stay focused on the road ahead. That's not all. Model Prime continues to innovate with efficiency-enhancing features on the outside. Take the available solar-charging roof, for instance. This system improves Model Prime's efficiency by charging the hybrid battery when parked.
On the move, the energy accumulated is redirected to auxiliary equipment like the USB charging ports and climate controls, allowing the plug-in hybrid system to motivate the vehicle more effectively. Once inside, Model Prime's well-appointed cabin blends comfort and convenience with modern styling. This starts with a heated steering wheel on all grades. On the other hand, XSE brings a soft-text trimmed heated 8-way power adjustable driver's seat, while XSE Premium adds ventilated front seats, two-position memory for the driver, and available heated outboard rear seats. To complement its creature comforts, Model Prime comes standard with practical touches like 60-40 split fold-down rear seats and a lightweight tonneau cover that's easily stowed when not in use. Additionally, there's 20.3 cubic feet of cargo volume behind the rear seats, meaning Model Prime is as practical as it is stylish.
Model Prime's clean interior layout is cleverly integrated with the latest entertainment and convenience features. This starts with a standard 8-inch Audio Multimedia touchscreen on SE and XSE. The real showstopper, however, is the available 12.3-inch touchscreen. In either case, it's easy to tap into the latest generation Brand Audio Multimedia platform that includes standard wireless compatibility for Apple CarPlay, which connects drivers to the apps on their iPhone, including Maps, Messages, Podcasts, and Music subscriptions. In addition, wireless Android Auto compatibility makes it easy to stay connected to Android devices and access various apps and services from Google, including YouTube Music, Google Maps, and Google Assistant. Plus, there's a standard three-month SiriusXM PlatinumPlan trial subscription that includes over 165 channels available in cabin and even more on the SXM mobile app, making it easier than ever to enjoy not just ad-free music, but also sports, comedy, and more.
On select grades, Model Prime also includes trials to Brand 's Connected Services, including Remote Connect, Safety Connect, and the new Drive Connect, which includes cloud navigation, destination assist, and intelligent assistant functionalities. On the other hand, an available eight-speaker JBL Premium audio system lets drivers get the most out of Brand 's latest audio multimedia platform, along with six USB-C ports and an available slotted Qi-compatible wireless charger.
Model Prime's tech story doesn't stop there because it makes full use of the latest features to provide added convenience on the road. A top-bound 7-inch multi-information display keeps crucial information in the driver's sightlines, while standard Blind Spot Monitor with Rear Cross-Traffic Alert and available Front and Rear Parking Assist with Automatic Braking provide an additional layer of assistance on the road.
On the other hand, the available Panoramic View Monitor gives the driver a 360-degree perspective around the vehicle while maneuvering at low speeds. Finally, with a Drive Connect subscription, the available Traffic Jam Assist takes convenience to another level, enabling hands-free acceleration, braking, and steering with driver supervision under 25 miles per hour on controlled-access freeways.
Model Prime brings peace of mind thanks to Brand Safety Sense 3.0, a standard safety and convenience suite that takes the TSS platform to an even higher level. Enhancing systems like its Pre-Collision System with Pedestrian Detection, Lane Departure Alert with Steering Assist, Automatic High Beams, Full Speed Range Dynamic Radar Cruise Control, Lane Tracing Assist, Road Sign Assist, and Proactive Driving Assist.
When conditions are met, Proactive Driving Assist provides gentle braking into curves or gentle braking and or steering to help support driving tasks such as distance control between a preceding vehicle, pedestrian, or bicyclist. And naturally, Model Prime also features Brand 's signature Star Safety System and eight airbags throughout the cabin. Whether tapping into EV-only power around town or hybrid mode on road trips, Model Prime is the complete package.
