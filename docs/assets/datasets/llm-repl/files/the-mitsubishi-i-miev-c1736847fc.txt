Brand Motors i-MiNiCAR was launched in Japan in January 2006 and received high acclaim because of its unique styling and advanced packaging, resulting that year in awards such as the RJC Car of the Year, the Most Advanced Technology category in the Car of the Year Japan, and the Good Design Award.
We would like to introduce you to the Brand iMii, our new all-electric vehicle, which is being jointly developed with the Japanese Electric Power Company based on the concept of the i. The engine layout of the i is different from a conventional front-wheel drive car. It uses a rear midship layout with a mid-motor and rear drive. This is a unique new design with unprecedented space efficiency.
For the iMeave model, the rear midship layout continues, but instead of a gasoline tank, the iMeave's lithium-ion batteries are mounted onto the floor and the engine has been replaced with an electric motor. Thus, in this layout, an electric vehicle has been created. In conventional gasoline engine vehicles, the power created by the engine goes through the transmission and differential gears to drive the wheels of the vehicle.
In electric vehicles, the electrical power stored in the battery is used to drive the motor, which through the reduction gears in the differential drives the rear wheels. Unlike internal combustion gasoline engines, electric motors use energy extremely efficiently.
Electric vehicles do not produce any engine vibrations or exhaust gases, making it a unique, clean and fun-to-drive experience. Also, during deceleration, kinetic energy is captured and the motor is used to generate electricity to help recharge the battery. In the new iMeave, revolutionary new core technologies are used in both the battery and motor.
One is the high-density lithium-ion battery, which is a requirement of electric vehicles. There are 88 lithium-ion cells mounted on the car, which provide a cruising range of up to 155 km in optimal conditions on a single charge. The other is the newly designed electric motor that is very light and compact, yet offers excellent power output.
At any operation speed, it offers smoother acceleration than a gasoline engine. Power output is rated at 66 horsepower and 145 pound feet of torque. Recharging is very easy. From a 120 volt household socket, it takes approximately 22 hours from completely discharged. From a 240 volt EVSE, charging is completed in approximately 7 hours from completely discharged. You can recharge it at night while you sleep and be ready for your commute the next day.
If you use an external DC quick charger, recharging to 88% takes about 30 minutes. Brand Motors
