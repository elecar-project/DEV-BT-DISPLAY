Set for a spring 2009 release, the 2010 Brand Model Hybrid hopes to answer the fuel-efficient gauntlet thrown down by Brand. The Model is Brand 's response to the Brand Model, and like the Model, is expected to receive over 40 miles per gallon for both city and highway driving.
The Model uses a complete gas-electric hybrid system and features a 1.3-liter, 98-horsepower, 4-cylinder engine paired to an electric motor. When it comes to looks, the Model resembles the Model, but has a sleeker, sharper exterior. On the interior, the dashboard exudes a video game-esque feel. The speedometer turns from blue to green based on how efficiently you are driving, while the EcoGuide display lets you earn five digital plants with leaves that grow if you drive green enough. Basically, the Model will let you know what you can do to be as efficient as possible.
This April, low emissions, amazing fuel economy, futuristic interior design, and functionality coupled with a starting price under $20,000 makes environmentally conscious driving economically feasible.
