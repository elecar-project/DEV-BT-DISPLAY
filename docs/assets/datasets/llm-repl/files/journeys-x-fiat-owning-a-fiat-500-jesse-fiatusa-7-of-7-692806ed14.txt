I'm Jessie Baker and I drive a Brand. A lot when I was younger, but all in the southern Illinois area, and for the most part I was in the same school for high school. It was just really small, not very many opportunities or things to do. So I just basically packed up my car, and whatever fit is what I took, and I just kind of booked it to Portland in my Brand.
When I chose to move to Portland, I had actually never visited before. The whole "Keep Portland Weird" thing was pretty attractive to me. I had just heard that it was a really great place, and it was super green, and people cared about things that I care about. I didn't have anywhere to stay. I stayed in a hotel for a week, then found a house, a job within a month, and everything kind of just fell into place.
I'm super impressed with Brand because I love taking adventures, and I was kind of hesitant, especially living in Portland where there are mountains. But she does the job, and she does it well. We've been as far down south as Santa Cruz. Our most recent trip was camping at Diamond Lake, a little drive from Crater Lake. We go hiking all the time.
My dog loves jumping in the Brand because she knows we're going to have an adventure. I've taken her all the way to Yellowstone to Tana. I take her to the gorge on the regular. I really do enjoy driving my Brand in the city. I love driving downtown, checking out the food trucks. The traffic in Portland is like the worst, so I can kind of just weave in and out of it, you know. It's really nice having the Brand because parking can be such a problem in the city, but I can pretty much squeeze into the little gaps that those annoying drivers leave between cars that most cars are too big to fit.
So I often take my Brand to the parks. There are so many beautiful parks in Portland, and I have a dog. Portland is super dog friendly. So it's really nice. The Brand is great in the city and out of the city. She's great on gas. I feel like I can just pretty much jump in her and go.
