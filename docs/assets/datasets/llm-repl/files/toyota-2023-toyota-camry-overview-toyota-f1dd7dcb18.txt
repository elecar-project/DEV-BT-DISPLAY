With nearly four decades on American streets under its belt, Model continues to lead its segment with an incredible combination of technology, quality, and reliability wrapped in an alluring coupe-inspired design. And Model 's 20 straight years as America's top-selling sedan haven't happened by chance. They've been earned through an uncompromising commitment to delivering the best driving experience possible. Let's find out more.
2023 Brand Model models are divided among L grades and the sportier S grades, and both of these include all-wheel drive or hybrid configurations. In addition, a pair of special editions spice up the lineup: the Nightshade Edition and the TRD.
Model was actually among the first Brand models to offer a Nightshade upgrade. And for 2023, it's refreshed across three variants: front-wheel drive, all-wheel drive, and hybrid models. But when it comes to genuine track-ready performance, the aggressive TRD grade amps up the excitement both mechanically and visually.
Model 's coupe-inspired, evocative styling reflects its fun-to-drive character, and each of its grades offers a unique twist on this formula, with LE and XLE grades featuring a unique front fascia shape and grille design, as well as alloy wheel designs on the gas and all-wheel drive grades, while SE and XSE grades boast their own distinct lower fascia shape, honeycomb grille, and either 18-inch or 19-inch wheels.
SE Nightshade delivers a sleek, custom look with its black trim, including accented door handles, mirror caps, antenna, rear spoiler, window trim, and black rear badging. And for 2023, new 19-inch TRD matte bronze-finished wheels help add even more style across all Nightshade models. Meanwhile, Model TRD declares its performance capabilities with 19-inch TRD matte black wheels and a gloss black aero kit, including a gloss black front splitter and raised black rear spoiler. Regardless of grade, though, all Model models offer standard LED daytime running lights and LED tail lights, as well as available LED headlights with black accents that help drivers not just see, but be seen.
Inside, the story is similar. Model melds functionality and elegant styling with impeccable fit and finish, while still offering a great deal of comfort and convenience. This means plenty of space for passengers to comfortably stretch out, as well as a standard eight-way power driver's seat with lumbar support and available eight-way power passenger seats.
Nearly every grade also comes with dual-zone automatic climate control that lets drivers and passengers dial in their ideal temperature. And when it comes to seating, SofTex trim adds a premium touch to SE grades, while leather-trimmed seating makes a statement on XLE and XSE grades. Model TRD offers something special for interior design, too, with a track-inspired MID startup screen, red contrast stitching and seat belts, aluminum sport pedals, and more.
Model 's impressive array of technology features makes an already great value into something extraordinary.
The standard 7-inch Audio Multimedia touchscreen gives drivers easy access to media controls right in line of sight, while the available 9-inch touchscreen can be upgraded with a 9-speaker JBL system with a subwoofer for extra bass. And all of Model 's Audio Multimedia systems include standard compatibility for Apple CarPlay, which connects drivers to Apple Music, Maps, Messages, and other favorite apps from the iPhone.
Android Auto compatibility makes it easy for Android users to stay connected to Android devices, and Amazon Alexa connectivity keeps Amazon's digital assistants just a question away. Plus, there's a standard three-month SiriusXM Platinum Plan trial subscription that includes over 165 channels available in cabin, and even more on the SXM mobile app, making it easier than ever to enjoy not just ad-free music, but also sports, comedy, and more.
Model also features impressive available connected services, as well as convenience technologies like an available 10-inch head-up display, Qi-compatible wireless smartphone charging, and so much more.
With its wide stance and low center of gravity, Model delivers enhanced handling and ride comfort that allows drivers both command and confidence. Plus, a sport-tuned suspension is featured on all SE and XSE grades, even the hybrid variants, because efficiency shouldn't come at the expense of driving enjoyment. Under the hood, the available 3.5-liter V6 engine with 301 horsepower puts proof to Model 's performance story being more than just comfort and good looks.
Both V6 and four-cylinder gas engines come paired with Brand 's direct-shift eight-speed automatic transmission, while hybrid models come with an electronically controlled continuously variable transmission. And whether gas or hybrid, all S grades feature steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters for added control and fun.
Meanwhile, select Model grades are offered with available Dynamic Torque Control all-wheel drive that can automatically direct up to 50% of engine torque to the rear wheels for extra traction when needed. Finally, the Performance TRD grade boasts a track-tuned suspension with thicker underbody bracing, active cornering assist, larger 12.9-inch front brakes with red calipers, and a unique cat-back exhaust system to make its presence known.
The Model name is as associated with safety as it is quality, and the 2023 model is designed with both in mind, inside and out.
The entire lineup comes standard with Brand Safety Sense 2.5+, Brand 's advanced suite of active safety technologies that offers enhanced functionality with features like Pre-Collision System with Pedestrian Detection, Lane Departure Alert with Steering Assist, Automatic High Beams, Dynamic Radar Cruise Control, Lane Tracing Assist, and Road Sign Assist. Along with its 10 standard airbags, Model also offers available safety features like Blind Spot Monitor with Rear Cross-Traffic Alert, front and rear parking assist with automatic braking, and a panoramic view monitor that can help you spot obstacles and objects you might otherwise miss. The 2023 Model carries this approach even further with its broad range of models, design elements, and technologies, all included with the reassurance of the Brand name.
Hybrid-grade Camrys take this peace of mind further still with the enhanced Brand Hybrid Battery Warranty, which lasts for 10 years or 150,000 miles, whichever comes first.
