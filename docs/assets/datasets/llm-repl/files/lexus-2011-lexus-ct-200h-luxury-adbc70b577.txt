From ride quality to fit, finish, and the quality of materials, luxury has been a hallmark of Brand from the very start. And the CT200H carries on that tradition. Thanks in part to the acoustic glass windshield and extensive use of vibration isolating technologies, this new luxury subcompact delivers a Brand quality ride, quiet and smooth. Inside, engineers drew on a new material called NuLux for the standard interior upholstery trim, supple yet very durable. Nulux was chosen both for its leather-like feel and its environmental benefits. It weighs half as much as leather, generates 65% less CO2 in the production process, and requires no volatile organic compounds or metal-based dyes in its production. In terms of the interior, the cockpit-like layout has been designed with driver needs in mind, putting all critical driving controls within comfortable reach while driving.
The standard 10-way power adjustment driver's seat includes a Brand memory system with three position buttons that also control the outside mirrors. The interior offers choice as well, with four different accent trim materials available as dealer installed options. Those choices include standard textured black, black metallic, genuine ash burl wood, and genuine bamboo.
In addition to luxurious upholstery and trim, the rear passengers may notice their seats are more centered and slightly higher than the front to give second row occupants a better view of the road ahead. CT200H owners can also opt for one or more of the available luxury features, including an available one-touch open-close moonroof to let a little of the outdoors in, an available Brand premium audio system with 10 speakers and six CD changer.
An available backup monitor with its picture displayed in the rear view mirror or on the navigation screen in vehicles equipped with that feature. An available advanced parking guidance system offering visual and audio feedback as the vehicle approaches obstacles while backing up. An available electrochromic driver's mirror with compass that dims automatically when it senses headlamps approaching from the rear. Electrochromic outside mirrors are also available. Available rain sensing windshield wipers and an available HomeLink programmable garage door opener. Combined with outstanding materials, precision fit and rich finish, these comfort and convenience features represent the kind of luxury the world expects from a Brand.
