So let's explore why Model continues to be the innovative choice for fun-loving and eco-conscious drivers across the globe.
The 2022 Model offers six distinct takes on the hybrid experience, so drivers can find their ideal vehicle. First, L Eco has the best fuel efficiency estimates of the entire Model lineup, while also having the lowest starting price. From there, the LE, XLE, and Limited grades each bolster the feature list with impressive style, comfort, and convenience upgrades.

Finally, an all-new Nightshade Edition brings an array of blacked-out accents that give Model an extra dose of attitude and style, and it's available with AWD as well.
Around the world, Model is recognized for its fuel-efficient and iconic design, but it pushes the envelope even further with technological innovations that enhance the driving experience while also conserving resources.
Even the air conditioning system is designed for maximum efficiency, with a SmartFlow mode that only sends airflow to occupied seats. Meanwhile, all grades feature a touchscreen audio multimedia system that's compatible with Apple CarPlay, which connects drivers to Apple Music, Maps, Messages, and other favorite apps from your iPhone.
And most Model grades also include Android Auto compatibility, which makes it easy to stay connected to Android devices. Plus, all grades offer Amazon Alexa compatibility, which keeps Amazon's digital assistant just a question away. Select Model grades also add modern conveniences like Qi-compatible wireless charging and a smart key system for easy vehicle entry. But if that's still not enough, the available cargo crossbars on Model offer even more storage and possibilities for your next weekend escape. Brand has always put a premium on safety, so it's no surprise that the entire Model lineup has been designed for passenger and pedestrian wellbeing.
To this end, all Model models feature Brand Safety Sense 2.0. This robust safety suite includes a pre-collision system enhanced with low-light pedestrian detection and daytime bicyclist detection, lane departure alert with steering assist and road edge detection, automatic high beams, and full-speed range dynamic radar cruise control. TSS 2.0 also includes two cutting-edge safety systems not found on the previous Brand Safety Sense platforms, Lane Tracing Assist, which helps keep Model centered in its lane while DRCC is operating, and Road Sign Assist, which is designed to recognize certain traffic signs and display them on the instrument panel.
The entire Model family also includes Star Safety System, 10 airbags, an integrated backup camera to help see what's happening out back, and an available Blind Spot Monitor with Rear Cross-Traffic Alert system to help keep an eye on hard-to-see areas. Model is world-renowned for its fuel economy estimates, but it also excites drivers with remarkable performance, thanks in part to the immediate torque delivery of the hybrid electric powertrain's electric motor, but also the underlying TNGA platform and its independent rear suspension, perfect for when the roads get twisty.
That said, smart efficiency is still the main attraction for Model, and that applies to the AWD models as well.
