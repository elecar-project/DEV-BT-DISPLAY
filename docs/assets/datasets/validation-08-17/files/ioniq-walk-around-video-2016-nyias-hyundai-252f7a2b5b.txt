Thank you for joining us today for the North American unveil of the new Hyundai IONIQ. IONIQ is the world's first vehicle to be offered in three electrified powertrains. It will be available as a hybrid with a class-leading fuel economy, a plug-in hybrid that could travel more than 25 miles on electric power, and also provide the efficiency of a conventional hybrid and an electric vehicle that has a range of 110 miles, which is the highest of any compact electric vehicle that is currently in the marketplace. By offering three different powertrains,
We're providing customers the power of choice and allowing them to decide which alternative powertrain option best fits their automotive needs. Because the IONIQ represents Hyundai's first dedicated alternative fuel vehicle, we engineered it to provide efficient operation without compromising on the vehicle's driving dynamics. The electric motor is powered by a state-of-the-art lithium-ion polymer battery system, while the six-speed dual-clutch transmission provides crisp, responsive shift feel.
and sophisticated multi-link rear suspension provides for superior ride quality and stability. The IONIQ delivers a unique, contemporary design that has been influenced by efficiency and technology, achieving an industry-low drag coefficient of 0.24. The interior of IONIQ projects a futuristic environment with an intuitive, clutter-free layout, and the eco-friendly aspect of the vehicle is reflected by the recycled plastic and plant-based bio-fabric materials
used throughout the interior. Technology is also used to deliver comprehensive safety by offering an entire suite of advanced active safety features such as automatic emergency braking with pedestrian detection, lane departure warning, and blind spot detection with rear cross traffic alert.
