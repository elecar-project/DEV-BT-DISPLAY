The new Lexus UX is a combination of the brand's innovative luxury and safety wrapped in a bold design and geared by ultra-efficient new powertrains. A new genre urban crossover with a compact design, bold and stylish. The UX has a unique Lexus identity, deeply rooted in the YET philosophy. YET is about combining seemingly opposite concepts into innovative solutions.
For UX it means a design providing the sense of security of a crossover yet the agile handling of a hatchback. UX is stylish yet functional with its wheel arches, rear combination lights and wheels all having an aerodynamic function. Dynamic yet practical.
Thanks to its low centre of gravity, UX provides excellent driving performance and this is all made possible thanks to the new TAC platform implemented by Lexus for the first time with UX, giving UX fantastic handling agility, ride comfort and a fun driving personality. UX is also equipped with brand new powertrains, smooth and responsive, yet very fuel efficient.
UX comes with second-generation suite of advanced safety features, Lexus Safety System Plus, and is already open for the first orders. First delivery is expected early next year. Very exciting!
