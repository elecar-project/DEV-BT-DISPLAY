Introducing the 2018 Hyundai Elantra. Designed to give you what you really want in a compact. More. The Elantra's exterior displays an evolution of the design language Hyundai has become famous for. Called fluidic sculpture to create a new, more refined expression called dynamic precision. Which aims to blend aspects of dynamism with a sense of balance. The thoughtfulness we've given to the outside of the Elantra continues inside.
where you'll find contemporary and intuitive interior design along with a total interior volume of 110 cubic feet. Forward thinking design is also on display in the Elantra's available HID headlights with dynamic bending light to help you see around corners. Available door handle approach lights help make entry at night easier and the Elantra's available LED daytime running lights and taillights help increase visibility to other drivers.
