At Lexis, we're always looking to push the boundaries of our advertising just a little bit more. And when we launched the previous Lexis ES, we used advertising that was completely written by AI. And this time we asked ourselves, could we use artificial intelligence in a different way? So we looked at how we could use AI to make a dynamic piece of advertising that could respond to the way you feel.
in real time and nothing like this had ever been done before but you know we like a challenge. Audio visual stimulation plays a huge role in shaping and creating our emotional experiences. In this project we are using techniques from effective computing, audio alchemy, and color psychology to generate physiological and psychological responses in users.
Using pioneering facial recognition software, we analyze the emotions of our users five times throughout the experience and then output different version of the film that matches the mood. Not only are we changing the visuals that the user is seeing throughout the experience, but we're also changing the soundtrack. In total, there are 3,000 possible combinations of content, creating an experience that is truly dynamic and personalized.
The story of this film is quite simple. It's about a guy that finishes a day of work in his house, takes his Lexus ES and drives around the city. Everything works towards the final scene where we see a wide open and uplifting viewpoint that shows our driver looking at this incredible city panorama and feeling relaxed at the end of a day of work.
This project was inspired by the Lexus ES, a car that uses various elements to create a feel-good factor for the driver. For example, the climate concierge that can adjust the temperature accordingly or even the way that the ride has been engineered to reduce eye fatigue. We felt a clever car required a clever piece of advertising and that's why the fit was perfect.
