The GT comes with an 800 volt system and a maximum charging power of 270 kilowatt. That allows us to recharge the GT from 5 to 80 percent in just 22.5 minutes. Important to realize that fast charging time is our intelligent thermal management system with four different cooling circuits. In extremely close collaboration with our designers we developed the surfaces of the vehicle so that they have an aerodynamic function but also an aesthetic function. We paid a lot of attention to the glazing concept, to the sealing concept, and also used optimized wing mirror in order to achieve the outstanding drag coefficient of 0.24, which is very, very good for a class of vehicle of this type.
