No matter if you desire fuel efficiency or something more powerful, the offers great options engineered to make every driver happy. The standard 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine produces 185 horsepower, making it ideal for your daily commute and all driving situations.
The Sport 2.0T and Limited 2.0T models are powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged GDI engine that's engineered for exhilarating performance, delivering 245 horsepower and 260 pound-feet of torque. This engine is paired with a standard 8-speed automatic with Shiftronic for a better range of performance while keeping fuel economy in mind.
For the utmost in fuel efficiency among the lineup, the Eco is powered by our Gamma 1.6-liter turbo GDI inline four-cylinder engine and is engineered to give you the perfect balance of fuel economy and performance. Featuring a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission, it produces 178 horsepower and superior off-the-line acceleration while delivering up to an EPA-estimated 37 miles per gallon highway.
