When it comes to power and comfort, driving the C-Class allows you to experience the best of both worlds. The entire C-Class model range now has new 4-cylinder engines. Available as petrol, diesel or plug-in hybrid variants, they all offer increased efficiency and power at the same time. Let me show you.
Every C-Class comes with an electrified drivetrain, either as a plug-in hybrid or a mild hybrid drive version like this one. It's basically a system that shuts down or starts your engine automatically depending on when you need it, allowing you to coast along flat or sloping roads and save fuel. And when braking, it recovers energy.
Of course, all shifting is carried out smoothly by ' renowned 9G-tronic automatic transmission, which now comes as standard for the first time in the C-Class. A perfect example of ' advance in technology is the rear-axle steering. You might have seen it already on the new S-Class. The same technology is now on the C-Class. In the city, it reduces the car's turning circle and helps to nail any tricky parking manoeuvres.
On curvy roads like these, boy, it just needs a bit of steering input to push the car through these bends.
