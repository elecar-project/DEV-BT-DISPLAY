Welcome to the new, introducing the new design language, characterized by the typical double-arch front grille with a 3D mesh, but also the new human-look headlights, full LED. The rear design introduces new taillights, new trunklets, and a new rear bumper. The big novelty is, of course, the introduction of the 2.2-litre four-cylinder diesel engine, developing 170 horsepower and 400 Newton-meters of torque, and emitting less than 130 grams. The direct response hybrid system also evolves for more performance and better efficiency.
