So the Folga Rail is for us a huge step towards. It's the first full electrical car that we launched to the market. And we, with the interior design, wanted to create something that is coherent. This evolutionary approach, together with the attention for the environment, led us to develop a completely new fabric made by Econyl Yarna.
Econyl is a fiber, is a regenerated nylon made by an Italian company that is made by recycling fishing nets in the sea and other plastic scraps in order to give a new opportunity, a new design opportunity also to what could be considered as a waste. You wouldn't expect that this could become beauty inside of a, but that's exactly an interesting part of our story.
If we talk about characteristics, Econyl is very robust and very resistant, but the touch is exactly the opposite. It's a very soft and gentle material, which is very, very interesting to touch, very different from any other materials that we are using right now in our interiors.
We were inspired by the natural phenomena of Aurora Borealis in which the exchange of energy is visible and you can see the power of nature.
