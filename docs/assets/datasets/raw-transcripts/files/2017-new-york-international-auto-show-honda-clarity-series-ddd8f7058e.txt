 Today we introduce the Honda Clarity line. We have a fuel cell powered Clarity, a pure battery electric Clarity, and a plug-in hybrid. We're able to develop a platform and virtually use the same body for all three incarnations of the car. The styling is meant to exude the look of modernness. The interior is designed to be modern and clean with a lot of flowing lines and very roomy. We have a lot of use of biofabrics in the car because people want to know that the materials in the car come from a renewable source and have a minimum carbon footprint. One of the commitments that we have is to electrify two-thirds of our automobile fleet by the year 2030. We could have picked an easy solution, but rather we chose to make one car work with three different modes of powertrain, and give people a choice.
