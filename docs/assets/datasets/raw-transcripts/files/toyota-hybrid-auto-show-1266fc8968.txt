 The 2008 Prius Hybrid. There are so many reasons to love this car. Exceptional gas mileage, cool design, and low emissions make my ride a Toyota Prius. Hybrid Synergy Drive is the industry leading hybrid technology, a perfect combination of a gasoline engine and an electric motor create an innovative way to power an automobile with a lower, more responsible level of emissions. An amazing combined estimated miles per gallon of 46 means that I spend less time in the gas lane and more time in the fast lane. I love that the mileage is high and the emissions are low. The Prius combines innovative engineering with stylish design. There is available Bluetooth navigation. I'm sorry, Bluetooth and DVD navigation and even a backup camera. The hybrid rides as good as it looks. The Toyota Prius moving forward.
