 The electrified GV70 from Genesis offers impressive features among three-row midsize SUVs with its abundant technology on board to fit your needs. Let's have a walk around the 2023 Genesis GV70 for a closer look at this SUV. Starting with the dual motors, the GV70 comes equipped with a 77.4 kWh battery that can generate 429 horsepower with an EPA-estimated 91 miles per gallon equivalent combined and 236 miles of range. The Genesis GV70 comes with a remote start system as well as push-button start and a fingerprint authentication system. Inside the GV70, you'll find a wireless phone charger and Wi-Fi hotspot. There's also three-zone climate control plus heated and ventilated front seats and heated steering wheel. The GV70 can comfortably seat up to five people and a hands-free automatic liftgate grants access to all their cargo. In terms of technology, the GV70 has a 14.5-inch touchscreen with embedded navigation and an available head-up display. Apple CarPlay and Android Auto come standard and a Lexicon premium sound system with 15 speakers is available. When it comes to standard safety, the GV70 is equipped with Lane Keep Assist with Blind Spot Mode, which is a feature that allows you to monitor your vehicle's driving behavior and the driver's driving behavior. The GV70 has a 3D surround parking camera to assist the driver with their surroundings. There's even available Remote Smart Parking Assist, which allows you to park your vehicle with the key fob. Moving to the exterior, you'll find 20-inch alloy wheels. Look a little higher to get a load of the heated power folding mirrors that can be controlled on the driver's side door panel. Atop the vehicle sits an available one-touch moonroof. At the rear of the vehicle, the LED taillights offer a modern and sporty design.
